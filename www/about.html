What I Do

Game Design

I call on knowledge and expertise in game design principles and trends to try and craft the best games out there. I’ve got a background in engagement learning and I’m a gamer at heart, fascinated with the systems that make them so darn fun.

Project
Development

Measure twice, code once. Finding the true needs of a project and communicating those with rest of the team is the foundation for a successful game. Nurturing strong systems of communication and organization enables a team to do better and produce their strongest work.

Code

I develop games in Javascript and C# specializing in the Unity Engine, but I’m comfortable jumping into unfamiliar territory and adapting to many other languages and libraries. I get jazzed up for pure game development, but have experience creating custom toolsets for projects as well.
