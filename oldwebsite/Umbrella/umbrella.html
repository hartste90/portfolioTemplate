UMBRELLA




     Umbrella is a fast paced Unity game where the player uses their umbrella to jump, float, and fight to survive enemies and even the harsh weather. Developed as a survival game experiment at UMBC's game development laboratory, this 10 week project utlized AGILE Game development techniques and weekly Sprint implementation. The team used Tortoise repository tools to manage the project and made sure each team member played an influential role in the development, beta, and eventual launch of our game.

     The project was introduced at UMBC's Undergraduate Research and Creative Achievement Day to wonderful support from players and the rest of the development community.



A very special thank you to the mentors that made this project possible: Dr. Marc Olano, Benjamin Walsh, and Yuzun Kang!
