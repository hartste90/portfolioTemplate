Coming Soon...
    • The player takes on the role of a haunted hotel in the 1920's. They must balance fear and energy resources to freak out visitors without making them leave.

    • As the player successfully operates their spooky hotel, more complex abilities and visitors will arrive, leading to surprisingly complex puzzles later on in the game.

    • Use the MOUSE to select a room in the hotel to enter it.
    • Use your abilities on the upper-left toolbar to scare, manipulate, and move the visitors.
    • Balance your fear and energy resources to create the scariest environment tailored to each visitor's greatest fears
    • Gain more powerful abilities, and don't stop until you have the spookiest hotel in the world!

    • Fearmonger is currently in Alpha stages.

    • Please keep checking in to see our progress as we add new abilities, visitors, and updated graphics and sounds!!
